## [0.1.0] – 2026-01-17
### Added
- Initial playable prototype of *Campaign Hero*
- Turn-based campaign loop with primary and general elections
- District generation with adjustable difficulty
- Candidate stats (charisma, discipline, empathy, stamina)
- Policy platform selection at game start:
    - Econ (socialist ↔ capitalist)
    - Social (liberal ↔ conservative)
    - Governance (legislative ↔ executive)
    - Tone (message-driven ↔ partisan attack)
- Per-demographic voter support model:
    - Working, College, Rural, Urban, Seniors, Youth
- Debate system with performance, momentum, and zinger virality
- Earned media and paid media mechanics
- Fundraising types (corporate, grassroots, mixed) with tradeoffs
- Canvassing / field operations
- Polling memo action showing demographic breakdown
- Action explanation menu with scheduled debate weeks
- Momentum, fatigue, and enthusiasm systems
- Scandal and volatility events
- End-of-phase election resolution

### Notes
- This release focuses on core systems and gameplay feel.
- Balance values are provisional and subject to tuning.
